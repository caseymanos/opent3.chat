US Employee Benefits Overview 2025

Health Insurance Benefits:
- Premium health insurance with low deductibles
- 100% coverage for preventive care
- Prescription drug coverage with $10 copays
- Dental insurance with annual cleanings covered
- Vision insurance including frames and contacts

Retirement Benefits:
- 401k plan with company matching up to 6%
- Vesting schedule: 100% vested after 3 years
- Additional pension plan for eligible employees
- Financial planning resources and advisors

Time Off Benefits:
- 20 vacation days per year (increasing with tenure)
- 12 sick days annually
- Personal days and mental health days
- Parental leave: 12 weeks paid for primary caregiver
- Bereavement leave and jury duty time off

Additional Perks:
- $2000 annual professional development budget
- Flexible work arrangements and remote work options
- Employee assistance program for personal issues
- Gym membership reimbursement up to $50/month
- Transit subsidies for public transportation
- Free snacks and beverages in office locations
- Annual company retreats and team building events

The total value of this benefits package is estimated at $15,000-$25,000 per employee annually, depending on usage and family size.
